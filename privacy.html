Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TND Studios ("we", "us") handles information in our games, including Mahjong Duel, Polygon Paradise, and our other titles (collectively, the "Games"). Some practices below apply only to certain Games, as noted. By using a Game, you agree to this policy.

Information we collect

We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run each Game. We do not ask for your real name, email address, or phone number to play.

  • Account identifier. Some Games (such as Mahjong Duel) create an anonymous account with a randomly generated ID when you first play. It is not tied to your personal identity.
  • Profile and gameplay data. Information such as a username you choose, your rating, wins and losses, match results, and your friends list.
  • Connection data. For online multiplayer, technical connection information (such as your IP address) is processed by our realtime networking provider for matchmaking and relaying game data.
  • Advertising and analytics data (ad-supported Games only). Some Games (such as Polygon Paradise) display third-party ads and use analytics. In those Games, our partners may collect a device advertising identifier and app-usage/diagnostic data to serve and measure ads and to understand how the Game is used. Ad-free Games (such as Mahjong Duel) do not include advertising, analytics, or tracking.

We do not collect precise location, contacts, or photos.

How we use information

We use information to operate and improve the Games: to create accounts, run online matches and matchmaking, maintain leaderboards and rankings, power the friends feature, keep the Games working and secure, and — in ad-supported Games only — to display and measure advertising. We do not sell your personal information.

Advertising (ad-supported Games)

Ad-supported Games (such as Polygon Paradise) use third-party advertising and analytics providers — for example Google AdMob and Google Analytics for Firebase — which may use a device advertising identifier and usage data to show and measure ads. You can limit ad personalization in your device settings (iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking / Apple Advertising), and where required we will ask for your consent before tracking. Ad-free Games do not use these.

Service providers

  • Google Firebase — accounts and database. See the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Photon / Exit Games — realtime multiplayer. See the Photon Privacy Policy.
  • Advertising/analytics partners (ad-supported Games only), such as Google AdMob and Google Analytics for Firebase. See the Google Privacy Policy.

Data retention and deletion

We keep your account data while your account exists. Where a Game supports accounts (such as Mahjong Duel), you can permanently delete your account and its associated data at any time from within the Game (Settings → Delete account). This removes your profile, username, and friends connections. Completed match records may be retained in anonymized, aggregate form as part of game history.

Children's privacy

The Games are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided information, contact us and we will delete it.

Security

We use reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date above when we do.
